Weekly Summary

The first race of the year is over and what a wild ride! The new regulations made for an exciting start to the season with technical and driver-management issues leading to multiple crashes and retirements over the course of the weekend. The qualifying order was shuffled quite a bit during the race, and although Max Verstappen started 20th of 22 cars on the grid, all of his overtaking during the race rewarded fantasy teams who featured him! Congrats to Fast Cars w/ Good Drivers for taking the first RACE WIN of 2026!

F1 Fantasy Rules (Quick Guide)

Each player builds a fantasy team made up of 5 drivers and 2 constructors within a $100M budget. Your team scores points based on how those drivers and teams perform during each race weekend.

  • Scoring: Points come from qualifying, race finish, positions gained, fastest lap, and bonuses.
  • DRS Boost: Each week you can apply a DRS Boost to one driver, doubling their race points.
  • Turbo Driver: Drivers under a price cap can receive a Turbo boost.
  • Transfers: Free transfers each race week; extra transfers may cost points.
  • Chips: Strategic chips (Limitless, Wildcard, etc.) can maximize scoring.
  • Deadline: Teams lock at the start of qualifying each race weekend.

Full rules and scoring details on the official F1 Fantasy site.
