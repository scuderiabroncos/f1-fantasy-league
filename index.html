F1 Fantasy Rules (Quick Guide)

Each player builds a fantasy team made up of 5 drivers and 2 constructors within a $100M budget. Your team scores points based on how those drivers and teams perform during each race weekend.

  • Scoring: Points come from qualifying, race finish, positions gained, fastest lap, and other performance bonuses.
  • DRS Boost: Each week you can apply a DRS Boost to one driver, doubling their race points.
  • Turbo Driver: Drivers under a certain price cap can receive a Turbo boost for extra points.
  • Transfers: You get free transfers each race week to change your lineup. Extra transfers may cost points.
  • Chips: Special chips (like Limitless or Wildcard) can be used strategically during the season to maximize points.
  • Deadline: Teams lock at the start of qualifying for each race weekend.

Full rules and scoring details can be found on the official F1 Fantasy site.
